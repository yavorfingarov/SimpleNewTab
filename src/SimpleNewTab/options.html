About

SimpleNewTab is a lightweight browser extension designed for efficiency and simplicity. It allows users to set shortcuts for their favorite websites. Additionally, SimpleNewTab fetches the daily Bing picture, providing a fresh background each day. With its minimalist design and privacy-friendly approach, SimpleNewTab streamlines the browsing experience without unnecessary clutter.

Privacy

This extension does not and will never collect any user information.
